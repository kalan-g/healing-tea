healing tea

about

Life gets busy.

It can get hectic and we often forget to take a moment for oursleves. Or, we end up feeling guilty for taking a day off. It's important to take a moment to breathe. Our bodies deserve it, and our minds need a moment to slow down.

Healing tea is here to remind you that it's okay to take a break. It's okay to spend 2 hours watching your favourite movie, or have a night out with friends. It's okay to just be and exist. It's okay to do absolutely nothing. We all need a moment to catch our breath. It's okay to relax

“Calm mind brings inner strength and self-confidence, so that’s very important for good health.”-Dalai Lama
